Cat FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

How long does it take to get an order?

Please allow 2 - 4 weeks for completion time. All of our products are handmade in our shop, and designed with your cat’s needs in mind.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we deliver. Shipping is available domestically, and if you’re local we offer white glove delivery for a nominal fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

VISA, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express.

Do you repair or recover Purrniture pieces?

Yes, we do routine upholstering of Purrniture. We also repair all types of cat furniture. Send us a picture and we can give you an estimate.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Yes, contact us for more details on our trade-in program.

Do you offer a guarantee?

We offer an exclusive four week Catisfaction Guarantee. If four weeks pass and your cat has not expressed interest in your purchase, simply return it for a full refund. We very seldom get a piece returned!
